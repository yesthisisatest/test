NOTABLE LUSTRON HOMES

LUSTRON HOMES are an architectural oddity that have captivated me since I first learned about them in an undergraduate Art History course. Their pastel color schemes inspired this proportional symbol map which explores the locations and numbers of remaining Lustrons across the United States.
