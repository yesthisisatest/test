THREE PERFECT DAYS IN VIROQUA

DURING MY TIME as a Landscape Intern at Taliesin Preservation, I developed a deep appreciation for the hilly landscapes of Wisconsin's Driftless Area. When given a "terrain representation" challenge in UW-Madison’s “Graphic Design in Cartography” course, I knew imediately that this was the landscape I wanted to work with. Designing point symbols to signify places of interest in and around the fly-fishing town of Viroqua, WI allowed me to draw on my studio art background.

Megan Roessler, Updated November 2018
