VISIT OREGON

WHEN CHALLENGED to design a tourism map in my "Introduction to Cartography" class, I was inspired by Pacific Northwestern forests and mountains. I ultimately decided to highlight the "Natural Wonders" of Oregon State, and used emerald green icons to call attention to these points of interest while giving a nod to the rich colors common across the Northwest.
